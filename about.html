ABOUT STUDENT FITNESS

Our Goal


     TOPIC: fitness and nutrition.
SITE NAME: https://studentfitness.com

DESCRIPTION: It’ll be a fitness app/ website specially designed for college
going students who find it very difficult to manage a fix
workout timetable and diet with their daily college routine.

(i)TARGETED AUDIENCE: all college going students
 hostelites and paying guests
 specified diet for vegetarians and jain.
 with and without supplements.

(ii)SITE AIM: to provide students with a guide that provides them with a
flexible workout and diet plan to meet their respective
fitness goals in
 mass gain
 fat loss
 shredding
 lean bulking etc…
that would be possible to be followed parallelly with their
day to day college schedule.

(iii)UNIQUENESS: Unlike other fitness apps that are designed keeping in mind
the major crowd of users that is the office going crowd and
elderly people this app is specially designed for teenagers and
college going students. It’ll be specially designed keeping in
mind a normal student’s daily schedule, diet,budget and
source availbilities

SITE DESCRIPTION: contain pages for:

 for workout plans for different muscle groups
 for different diet plans
 links directing to video tutorials on how to
perform particular exercises
 images for visual understanding
 a separate common comment/doubt section for
q&a to provide one to one solutions.
